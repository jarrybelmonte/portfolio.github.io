>> I. Formulaic Writing <<
The topic of this writing is my favorite activity during the Year Up orientation week. It includes a topic sentence, three concrete details (facts), three commentary (opinion) and a summary. This kind of writing is called formulaic writing.

>> II. Writing Professional E-mail <<
I am certain that this skill is very useful. When you write good e-mails, it just shows that you are an educated person.
A good e-mail includes a subject, greetings, the correct recipient, concise message, professional signature and making sure that it is proof-read.
